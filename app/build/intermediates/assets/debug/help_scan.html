In-store Beacon App Help

Provided that Bluetooth low energy is supported on the device the application will detect and show on the App the Beacons around it.

The user will see the products advertised by the Beacon and will have a button to access the Website with that promotion

The information that the user will see is the following:

  • Product discount with a discount percentage
  • Product brand being promoted
  • Product type in offer by this deal
  • Product Website whit more info about the deal
